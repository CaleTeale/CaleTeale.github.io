Photography
Ever since my first family holiday I have always had an interest in photography and certain techniques. Despite many years of taking photos, there is still much for me to learn, however, even through small sessions experimentating and testing different styles and subjects - I know I have an immense passion for the field of photography. My favourite technique (so far) is low light photography. Capturing the desired amount of light requires extreme patience, but the results a highly worthwhile and can exhibit many hidden meanings and stories.

My second favourite style is long exposure shooting. The result of this technique is an amazing effect. Even taking a simple photo of a car driving by can create awesome trail effects.
